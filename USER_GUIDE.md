## üåü Introduction

**SkillSwap** is a time banking platform where community members exchange services based on time rather than money. Every hour of service is valued equally, creating a fair and inclusive economy where everyone's contribution matters.

### Core Concept
- **1 Hour of Service = 1 Time Credit**
- All skills are valued equally
- Help others and earn time credits
- Use your credits to request services from others

## üìñ How to Use SkillSwap

### Step 1: Offer Your Services

1. **Navigate to Offer Service**
   - Click **"Services"** ‚Üí **"Offer Service"** in the navigation bar
   
2. **Fill in the Service Details**
   - **Title**: Give your service a clear, descriptive name
   - **Description**: Explain what you'll provide
   - **Category**: Select the appropriate category
   - **Available Hours**: How many hours you can offer
   - **Hourly Rate**: Set to 1 (standard for time banking)

3. **Submit Your Offer**
   - Click **"Submit Offer"**
   - Your service is now visible to all community members!

**Example Services:**
- Web Development (10 hours available)
- Guitar Lessons (5 hours available)
- Garden Maintenance (8 hours available)
- Math Tutoring (15 hours available)
- Cooking Classes (6 hours available)

### Step 2: Browse Available Services

1. **View All Services**
   - Click **"Services"** ‚Üí **"Categories"**
   - Browse by category or view all services
   
2. **Search for Specific Services**
   - Use the search functionality
   - Filter by category
   - Check available hours and ratings

3. **View Service Details**
   - Click on any service to see full details
   - Check the provider's profile and ratings
   - Read service description carefully

### Step 3: Request a Service

1. **Select a Service**
   - Find the service you need
   - Check if provider has available hours
   
2. **Make a Request**
   - Enter the number of hours you need
   - Click **"Request Service"**
   - Wait for the provider to accept or reject
   
3. **Track Your Request**
   - View status in your dashboard
   - Check **"Service Requests"** section
   - You'll be notified when the provider responds

### Step 4: Respond to Incoming Requests

1. **Check Your Dashboard**
   - View **"Incoming Requests"** section
   - See who needs your services
   
2. **Review Request Details**
   - Click **"View Details"** to see full information
   - Check requester's profile and rating
   
3. **Accept or Reject**
   - **Accept**: Time credits will be transferred
   - **Reject**: Requester will be notified
   
4. **Complete the Service**
   - Coordinate with the requester
   - Provide the agreed service
   - Both parties can leave reviews

## ‚ùì FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How much does SkillSwap cost?**
A: SkillSwap is completely free! You only exchange time, not money.

**Q: Are all services worth the same?**
A: Yes! One hour of any service equals one time credit. This creates equality in our community.

**Q: What if I run out of time credits?**
A: Offer your services to earn more credits. Everyone has valuable skills to share!

**Q: Can I use SkillSwap for my business?**
A: SkillSwap is designed for community skill sharing, not commercial services.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Contact support for username changes.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to your profile settings and select "Delete Account."

### Service Questions

**Q: What happens if someone doesn't complete a service?**
A: Report the issue to administrators. Credits can be refunded for incomplete services.

**Q: Can I offer multiple services?**
A: Yes! Offer as many services as you can provide.

**Q: How long does a request stay pending?**
A: Requests stay pending until the provider accepts or rejects them.

**Q: Can I cancel a request?**
A: Yes, you can delete pending requests from your dashboard.

### Credits Questions

**Q: Do time credits expire?**
A: No, your credits never expire!

**Q: Can I transfer credits to another user?**
A: Credits transfer automatically when services are accepted.

**Q: What's the maximum balance I can have?**
A: There's no limit to how many credits you can accumulate.

**Q: Can I withdraw time credits as money?**
A: No, credits can only be used for services within the platform.
